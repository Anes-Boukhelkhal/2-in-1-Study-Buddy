# 2-in-1-Study-Buddy: Ever wondered why you couldn't get work done even though you wanted to? The 2-in-1 Study Buddy by Anes and Alex might be a good fit for you!

Hardware: STM32 Nucleo F446RE Microcontroller (ENCLOSURE UNDER DEVELOPMENT)

<img width="800" height="800" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d2fddd6c-4350-430c-bfcc-4b83e5666934" />

How it works: 
1. When you start a study session, open the lid of the enclosure and insert your phone.
2. Press the [time up] button 4 times to light up 4 LEDs, each corresponding to a 15 minute timer for a total of 1 hour.
3. If you desire to study for less than 1 hour, quiz yourself on the material by answering a set of questions as you like. If you answer 4 questions correctly in a row by pressing the [correct] button, one of the LEDs turns off, so the study timer is decremented by 15 minutes.
4. Once you complete your study session either when the hour runs out or when you answered enough questions correctly, the enclosure unlocks for you to use your phone again.

Happy studying!
